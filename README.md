MarketFlow: Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes historical sales data for **MarketFlow** an international e-commerce company using **Microsoft Excel**.  
The objective was to explore **sales performance, customer demographics, and product category trends** in order to identify key business insights and revenue drivers.

The project covers the **full** analysis workflow:
- Data cleaning and preparation
- Exploratory data analysis
- KPI development
- Dashboard creation
- Business insights generation

## Key KPIs
- **Total Revenue:** ~$10M  
- **Total Orders:** ~60,000  
- **Time Period:** 2020 – 2025  


## 📊 Key Business Insights

### 🌎 Global Market Performance

- The **United States is the dominant market**, generating the highest order volume across all customer demographics.
- **US Adults lead with ~5,000 orders**, followed by **US Senior Citizens (~4,000)** and **Young Adults (~2,200)**.
- Several countries demonstrate moderate but consistent activity:
  - **Great Britain**
  - **Indonesia**
  - **Germany**
  - **France**
- Smaller markets such as **Poland, Sweden, and the UAE** show comparatively lower order activity.

💡 **Insight:** The company’s demand is heavily concentrated in a few core markets, particularly the **United States**.

---

### 👥 Customer Demographics

Customer purchasing behavior shows a consistent demographic hierarchy:

1. **Adults – highest order volume**
2. **Senior Citizens – second highest**
3. **Young Adults – moderate contribution**
4. **Adolescents – minimal purchasing activity**

- Adults dominate across **nearly all countries and product categories**.
- Interestingly, **Young Adults in the United States alone generate similar or higher order volumes than entire demographics in several other countries**.

💡 **Insight:** Older demographics are the **primary drivers of revenue and purchasing activity**.

---

### 🎯 Loyalty Program Utilization

Analysis of loyalty discount usage reveals consistent behavior across discount tiers.

- **Adults are the top users of the loyalty program across all discount levels (5%, 10%, 15%, and 20%)**
- **Senior Citizens rank second in loyalty utilization**
- **Young Adults show moderate engagement**
- **Adolescents show minimal participation**

💡 **Insight:** Loyalty engagement appears **demographic-driven rather than discount-driven**, with Adults and Seniors consistently using the program regardless of discount level.

---

### 🛒 Product Category Performance

Top performing product categories:

| Category | Revenue | Orders |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Books | ~$2M | ~13K |
| Toys | ~$1.7M | ~11K |
| Beauty | ~$1.5M | ~10K |

Mid-performing categories:

- **Fashion (~8K orders)**
- **Home & Kitchen (~7K orders)**
- **Sports (~6K orders)**

Lowest performing category:

- **Electronics (~3K orders)**

💡 **Insight:** Books and Toys together generate a **significant share of total company revenue**.

---

### 💰 Revenue Overview

- **Total Revenue:** ~$10M  
- **Total Orders:** ~60K  
- **Company Founded:** Early 2020  

Revenue distribution shows:

- Heavy **dependence on the US market**
- Strong concentration in a **small number of product categories**
- **Adults driving the majority of purchases**

---

### 📌 Strategic Takeaways

Based on the analysis:

- Prioritize **US market growth and retention**
- Target **Adults and Senior Citizens** in marketing campaigns
- Expand successful categories such as **Books and Toys**
- Investigate underperforming areas like **Electronics and adolescent engagement**

### Revenue Trends
- Sales reached their **highest peak in September 2025**, with nearly **$200K in monthly revenue**.
- The previous major peak occurred in **December 2022**, generating over **$150K**.
- This suggests strong **seasonal demand and continued revenue growth over time**.

### Customer Demographics
The **United States dominates order volume across all age groups**:
- **Adults:** ~5,000 orders
- **Seniors:** ~4,000 orders
- **Young Adults:** ~2,200 orders


Interestingly, **young adults in the US alone generate similar or higher order volumes than entire age segments in other countries**, highlighting the importance of this demographic.


## Dashboard Features
The Excel dashboard allows users to:
- Track revenue trends over time
- Compare sales performance by country
- Analyze order distribution by customer age group
- Identify top-performing product categories

---

## Business Value
This analysis highlights key opportunities for the company:
- Focus marketing efforts on **high-performing US demographics**
- Expand successful product categories such as **Books and Toys**
- Investigate seasonal spikes to optimize **inventory and promotions**
